### Instruction:
If a table employees has columns salary and bonus, calculate the total compensation for each employee.


### Response:
Let's think step by step.
1. Identify the table: employees.
2. Identify relevant fields: salary, bonus.
3. Determine the computation: salary + bonus.
4. Define column alias: AS total_compensation.
5. The SQL structure: SELECT salary + bonus AS total_compensation FROM employees;


### Instruction:
From the orders table, find the average order amount per customer.

### Response:
1. Identify the table: orders.
2. Identify fields: customer_id, order_amount.
3. Determine aggregation: AVG(order_amount).
4. Group by customer_id.
5. Structure: SELECT customer_id, AVG(order_amount) AS avg_amount FROM orders GROUP BY customer_id;
